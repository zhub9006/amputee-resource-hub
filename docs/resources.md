### Phase & Sponsor Analysis

**Phases:** All **Phase NA** — standard for device/procedural interventions (ISO 14155, FDA 21 CFR Part 812).

**Sponsor ranking (most promising):**
1. **Academic Medical Centers** — Shirley Ryan, Brigham & Women's, Duke, UW, VA — publish openly, patient assistance

3. **Government/DoD** — U.S. DoD, VA

**Recommendation:** Academic + NIH/DoD partnerships are most promising for affordable innovation.

- **Coverage:** Pharmacies within 1.5–2.5 km

### 🚇 Public Transit

**CTA Red Line** (wheelchair accessible): Cermak-Chinatown (~1.5 km), Sox-35th (~2.0 km), 35th-Bronzeville-IIT (~2.3 km)
**CTA Bus:** Halsted (Routes 8, 18, 108), State (Routes 4, X4), Cermak (Route 3)
**Metra:** Halsted St Station (~1.8 km) — ⚠️ NOT wheelchair accessible
**Water Taxi:** Chinatown terminal (~1.2 km); wheelchair accessible
**ADA Paratransit:** Metropolitan Transit Access — 1-888-968-7282

**Gaps for Amputees:**
1. Limited bus stops within Bridgeport — car-dependent
2. Halsted/Ashland Red Line stations underground without wheelchair access
3. CTA paratransit essential during Chicago winters

### 🚶 Walkability & Mobility

- Curb cuts at Halsted, State, Archer intersections
- Rest stops very limited — Half Acre park (0.5 km) has benches
- Shirley Ryan ~1.5 km from nearest Red Line — manageable for wheelchair users

## Part 4: Key Takeaways

### Clinical Trial Insights
1. **Affordable is emerging** — ReMotion $80 knee and Shirley Ryan $80 socket meet safety standards
2. **Academic + NIH/DoD partnerships** are most promising — publish openly, patient assistance
3. **Remote rehabilitation** (MDORP) validated as cost-reducing model
4. **Osseointegration** may eliminate sockets — addresses #1 abandonment cause

### Bridgeport Neighborhood Insights
1. **Healthcare strong** (6.9/10) — Shirley Ryan anchors the area
2. **Transit workable** — Red Line + 3 accessible stations; CTA paratransit for winter
3. **Parks = biggest gap** (1.0/10) — Plan outdoor rehab at Riverwalk + Ping Tom
4. **Fitness excellent** (8.9/10) — Shirley Ryan on-site fitness is gold standard
5. **Affordable + diverse** living with strong community
