## 🏥 Care Center Profile — Shirley Ryan AbilityLab, Chicago

**Location:** 345 E. Superior Street & 3737 S. Damen Ave, Chicago, IL 60611/60608
**Coordinates:** 41.8500°N, 87.6501°W

Shirley Ryan AbilityLab is the **#1-ranked rehabilitation hospital in the US** (U.S. News & World Report). Study site for **NCT04725461** (low-cost foam socket trial), and home to the **THOUGHT Lab** for bionic control and myoelectric prosthetics.

**Why Selected:**
- Lead site for NCT04725461 — affordability-focused for underserved amputees
- #1 US rehab hospital — consistently top-ranked
- THOUGHT Lab — bionic control, myoelectric prosthetics, neural decoding
- Comprehensive amputee rehab: inpatient & outpatient PT/OT, prosthetic training, peer mentoring
- Affiliated with Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine
- Accessible Bridgeport/Streeterville location with robust public transit

## 🔬 Key Takeaways

1. **Affordability is trending** — 3D-printed sockets, low-cost foam sockets, and body-powered sensory-prosthetic fingertips actively reducing costs
2. **Neuromorphic control is the frontier** — MIRA (NCT05768802) and powered ankle prostheses (NCT07645729) represent cutting-edge intuitive use
3. **Pain prevention matters** — PreventPAP (n=203) is the largest multicenter RCT for TMR vs neurectomy
4. **Global health innovation** — Gaza QoL study and 3D-printing trials in Cambodia/Tanzania/Uganda
5. **Bridgeport is ideal** — walkability (10), transit (9), 24/7 pharmacy, flat terrain
