## 📊 Clinical Trial Findings — Affordable Prosthetic Devices

Studies 5 and 8 represent the frontier of powered prosthetics and sensory feedback technology. Studies 6 and 9 provide critical evidence for cost-effectiveness and economic evaluation that directly affect device selection decisions. Study 10 validates a functional assessment tool that clinicians can use to track real-world outcomes.
