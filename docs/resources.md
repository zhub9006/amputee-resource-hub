## 🔑 Key Takeaways

1. **Affordability is trending** — 3D-printed sockets, low-cost foam sockets (NCT04725461), and body-powered sensory-prosthetic fingertips (NCT05220553) actively reducing costs
2. **Neuromorphic control is the frontier** — MIRA (NCT05768802), powered ankle prostheses (NCT07645729), and AMI (NCT03913273) represent cutting-edge intuitive use with NIH funding
3. **Pain prevention matters** — PreventPAP (NCT06719245, n=203) is the largest multicenter RCT for TMR vs neurectomy; actively recruiting
4. **ML/AI integration accelerating** — NCT06886295 (Bologna) validates machine-learning myoelectric control in unsupervised home settings
5. **Global health innovation** — Gaza QoL study (NCT07519746) and 3D-printing trials in Cambodia/Tanzania/Uganda
6. **Bridgeport is ideal** — walkability (10/10), transit (9/10), 24/7 pharmacy, flat terrain, 3 major hospitals, 17+ bus stops, Water Taxi access
7. **Active recruitment opportunity** — Four studies currently recruiting (NCT06886295, NCT06719245, NCT07645729, NCT05768802) — patients near Chicago should consider eligibility
