## Clinical Trial Findings — Affordable Prosthetic Devices

We searched ClinicalTrials.gov for recent studies on affordable prosthetic devices and limb loss rehabilitation, identifying **10 key studies** from a broader pool of 348 matching studies.

| # | NCT ID | Title | Status | Location | Focus |
|---|--------|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | NCT04725461 | Low Cost Prosthetic Socket | Completed | Chicago, IL (Shirley Ryan) | Expandable rigid-foam socket; low-cost transtibial |
| 2 | NCT04953364 | SMART Rehab | Recruiting | Vancouver, BC (UBC) | mHealth peer-supported rehab platform |
| 3 | NCT04904003 | ProMob | Completed | Oslo, Norway | Rehab elements influencing mobility |
| 4 | NCT03296904 | CYBERLEGs Plus Plus | Completed | Florence/Brussels | EU-funded powered ortho-prosthesis |
| 5 | NCT02891707 | MDORP | Unknown (was Active) | DC/Miami (VA/DoD) | Web-based mobile rehab for veterans |
| 6 | NCT04588753 | Hamstring Flexibility | Completed | Lahore, Pakistan | PT for trans-tibial amputees |
| 7 | NCT03409133 | Neural Feedback | Recruiting | Cleveland, OH (VA) | Sensory neuroprosthesis; implanted electrodes |
| 8 | NCT06415955 | AMPUTEEFACT | Recruiting | Nimes, France | Biomechanical & psychological factors |
| 9 | NCT06105944 | Economic Evaluation | Completed | Groningen, Netherlands | Cost-effectiveness: NMPK vs MPK knees |
| 10 | NCT05444868 | Glittre ADL Test | Completed | Nicosia, Cyprus | Validated functional assessment |

> **Key takeaway:** NCT04725461 (Shirley Ryan, expandable foam socket) and NCT02891707 (VA/DoD, free for veterans) are the most affordable. NCT03409133 (VA, sensory neuroprosthesis) is the frontier of feedback tech. Additional: NCT04700085 ($80 ReMotion Knee) and NCT02542761 (Mayo Clinic).
