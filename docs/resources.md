## 🏥 Care Center Profile

### Shirley Ryan AbilityLab — Chicago, Illinois
**Address:** 355 East Erie Street, Streeterville, Chicago, IL 60611
**Coordinates:** 41.8939 N, -87.6184 W

Shirley Ryan AbilityLab is the world's #1 ranked rehabilitation hospital (U.S. News & World Report) and is the leading center for prosthetic and rehabilitation research in the United States. It was the site for Study #1 (NCT04725461) on 3D-printed prosthetic sockets. The AbilityLab pioneered the protocol that cuts socket fabrication from days to under 90 minutes.
